Who am I?

I’m not just a robot – I’m your personal health companion. Inspired by the loyal nature of a medical assistance dog, I was created to make everyday life easier for people with special health needs. I’m small, round, and friendly by design – compact and mobile, always moving quietly and blending naturally into your life without being intrusive.

Whether you’re at home, in a care facility, or traveling the world, I’ll be right there with you, wherever you need me. My mission? To care for you. To keep you safe. To support you. And to do it all in a way that’s not only practical, but also brings you comfort and peace of mind.

What can I do?

I’m equipped with a wide range of sensors that monitor your vital signs in real time – including heart rate, oxygen levels, glucose, and skin conductivity. I also track environmental factors like air quality, temperature, and noise. With the help of a smart wristband, I create a detailed picture of your physical state. I use machine learning to recognize patterns and adapt to your individual needs.

In critical situations, I act instantly: I can administer insulin or alert medical services if you’re unable to respond. I also detect early signs of psychological stress, like panic attacks or dizziness, and respond with calming voice guidance or by bringing you water or medication.

My two robotic arms are both precise and gentle – allowing me to pick up and deliver small objects like medicine, bottles, or snacks. You can control me easily using voice or gestures. I’m always ready – with emergency protocols, GPS tracking, and direct connection to medical support.
